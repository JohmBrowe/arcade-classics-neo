What we collect

  • Anonymous analytics: We use Cloudflare Web Analytics (no cookies) to count page views and basic usage.

  • Analytics help us improve performance and game quality.
  • Your email enables magic-link sign-in. Your PIN and early-player code let us verify you later (e.g., to unlock ad-free).

We don’t sell your data. We only use Cloudflare (analytics) and Supabase (auth/database) to operate the site.

Data retention & deletion
